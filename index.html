Common questions

Are all Sage Systems Lab products offline-first?

No. Product formats vary. HTML tools and printable PDFs can be used offline after download in supported environments. Google Sheets templates require Google Sheets and a free Google account for primary use. Each listing states its format before purchase.

Do these tools connect to my bank?

No. The finance tools are manual-entry products and do not connect to bank accounts.

Do I need an account?

It depends on the format. HTML tools and PDFs do not require a Sage Systems Lab account. Google Sheets templates require a free Google account because they run in Google Sheets.

Are these financial advice?

No. These are planning and organization tools only. They do not provide financial advice, guarantee savings, guarantee payoff timelines, or replace professional advice.

Is the Extra Payment Loan Planner a mortgage calculator?

No. It is a fixed-rate loan payoff planner. It can estimate extra-payment impact for simple fixed-rate loans, but it does not model PMI, escrow, property taxes, insurance, variable rates, refinancing, or lender-specific fees.

Is the ADHD Planner a medical tool?

No. It is a printable planning layout designed for low-friction organization. It is not a medical device, treatment, therapy replacement, or diagnostic tool.
